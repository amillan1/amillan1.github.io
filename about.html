ABOUT ME

ANA K. MILLAN

I am a senior at Eastern Michigan University studying marketing with a minor in graphic communications. I am expected to graduate in April 2023. My drive and determination are attributes that characterize who I am. As a first-generation student pursuing a marketing degree, I hope to demonstrate my innovative ideas while experimenting with different mediums and, most importantly, learning from others.

 

In pursuit of my marketing passion, I am currently the Vice President of the American Marketing Association, where I have the opportunity to network and obtain fresh insights that will benefit my field. When I graduate, I want to look back on the new experiences and opportunities that let me leave my creative stamp on the world.
